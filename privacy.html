Legal · Privacy

Privacy policy.

We collect the minimum information needed to reply to inquiries and keep the site running. No tracking, no profiling, no ad-tech.

EFFECTIVE · May 25, 2026 · LAST UPDATED · May 25, 2026

1. Who we are

Blueforge AI is an industrial data intelligence consultancy. This policy covers blueforgeai.com and any messages you send to info@blueforgeai.com. We act as the data controller for the information described below.

2. What we collect

From the contact form

  • Your name and work email
  • Company / site name
  • Your primary historian and areas of interest
  • The message you write

We use this information to reply to your inquiry. We do not enrich it from third-party sources, sell it, or pass it to ad networks.

Automatically

  • Standard server access logs (IP address, request URL, user agent, timestamp) retained for ≤ 30 days for security and abuse prevention.
  • The site sets no first-party analytics cookies and loads no third-party analytics.

From third-party providers

  • Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com, fonts.gstatic.com). Google receives the request and your IP address; see Google's privacy policy.
  • Form delivery, when configured, uses a transactional form provider that receives the fields you submit.

3. How long we keep it

  • Contact-form messages: kept in our inbox for as long as commercially useful, then archived or deleted on request.
  • Server logs: ≤ 30 days.
  • If we begin a project together, contract-related correspondence is retained for the period required by accounting and tax law (typically 7 years).

4. Your rights

If you are in the EU/UK or another jurisdiction with similar laws, you have the right to access, correct, export, or delete any personal information we hold about you, and to object to its processing. Email info@blueforgeai.com with the subject line "Privacy request" and we will respond within 30 days.

5. Cookies

We use no advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies. The site stores a small amount of state in localStorage (your design-tweak preferences, if any) — this never leaves your browser.

6. Security

The site is served over HTTPS with HSTS, a strict Content-Security-Policy, and modern transport-security headers. Inbound email is filtered. We do not store payment information.

7. Children

The site is intended for industrial-sector professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16.

8. Changes

If we materially change how we handle personal information, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above. Substantive changes affecting existing inquiries will be communicated by email.

9. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints: info@blueforgeai.com. If you believe we have not handled your information properly, you may also contact your local data-protection authority.
